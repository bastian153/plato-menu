# How Plato works for a restaurant owner

## The sales pitch (2 minutes at their counter)

1. You open **their** demo on your phone: `/m/taqueria-el-sol` (or a blank new account).
2. Tap **Español** (or 中文) — descriptions switch.
3. Open a dish — real photos, clear price.
4. Say: *“Guests scan a QR. You type the menu once. We translate. You pick a vibe. Live same day.”*

They don’t install an app. Guests don’t either.

---

## Owner journey (how easy is it?)

### Goal: live QR on tables the same day

| Step | What they do | Time |
|------|----------------|------|
| **1. Account** | Email + password (or magic link) | 1 min |
| **2. Basics** | Restaurant name, emoji, hours | 1 min |
| **3. Vibe** | Tap one of 8 looks (taco, ramen, café…) | 30 sec |
| **4. Dishes** | Name, description, price, optional photo → **Translate all** → Save | ~1–2 min per dish |
| **5. Languages** | Turn on Spanish / Chinese / etc. | 30 sec |
| **6. Go live** | Print QR → stick on table / window | 5 min |

**Hard parts we remove**

- No reprinting paper when prices change  
- No writing 15 languages by hand  
- No hiring a designer (vibe presets)  
- No app store  

**Still manual (on purpose for v0.3)**

- They type dish names/prices (or you type for them as setup service)  
- They take/upload photos (or use guest photos later)  

**Built for easy onboarding**

- **Scan paper menu** (photo + AI if `XAI_API_KEY`, or paste text)  
- **Fast add 5 dishes** at once + auto-translate  
- **Multiple restaurants** per account (you manage many clients)  

**Still later**

- Import from Toast / Square  
- You (agency) $99 setup fee productized as a checklist

## What “vibe” means

Each restaurant picks a **theme**:

- Colors (accent, background, cards)  
- Mood name (Sunset taco, Night ramen, Espresso bar…)  

Guest menu uses those colors so a sushi spot doesn’t look like a BBQ joint.

---

## Your role when selling

**Best first offer:** “I’ll load your menu this afternoon. You approve. We put QR on tables tonight.”

That is:

1. Create their account  
2. Add 8–20 dishes + photos  
3. Translate  
4. Pick vibe with them  
5. Print QR  

Then monthly: $39 truck / $99 restaurant (planned pricing).
